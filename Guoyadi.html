Trade Works , Tariffs Don't

The U.S. needs free and fair trade, but imposing tariffs to get there is a wrong way .

Cooperation between China and the United States is the only way to win a win-win situation. A trade war will only bring harm.

（1）tariffs are inflicting harm on the American economy

After Trump begun to raise tariffs across countries. some of them have taken correspondingretaliatory measures.China ,Mexico ,EU and Canada have already retaliated or announced plans to retaliate with billions of dollars in tariffs on American-made products.This cannot be ignored because three of these countries are among the top five export markets of the United States.

（2）Trade with China promotes American Development

According to Oxford Economics, the commercial relationship with China supports 2.6 million jobs in the U.S. in 2017.
